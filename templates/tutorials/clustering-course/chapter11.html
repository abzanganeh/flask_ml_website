Chapter 11: DBSCAN - Density-Based Spatial Clustering

Density-based clustering with noise detection

DBSCAN Algorithm

DBSCAN (Density-Based Spatial Clustering of Applications with Noise) is a density-based clustering algorithm that can find clusters of arbitrary shape and identify noise points.

Key Parameters

  • eps (ε): Maximum distance between two points to be considered neighbors
  • minPts: Minimum number of points required to form a dense region
