Chapter 14: Clustering Evaluation Metrics and Validation Techniques

Measuring clustering quality and performance

Clustering Evaluation

Evaluating clustering results is challenging because we often don't have ground truth labels. This chapter covers both internal and external validation metrics.

Internal Validation Metrics

  • Silhouette Score: Measures how similar an object is to its own cluster vs other clusters
  • Davies-Bouldin Index: Ratio of within-cluster scatter to between-cluster separation
  • Calinski-Harabasz Index: Ratio of between-cluster to within-cluster dispersion

External Validation Metrics

  • Adjusted Rand Index (ARI): Measures similarity between true and predicted clusters
  • Normalized Mutual Information (NMI): Information-theoretic measure of cluster similarity
  • Homogeneity and Completeness: Measures cluster purity and coverage
